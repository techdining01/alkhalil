About

Al‑Khalil Masjid & Islamic Foundation advances worship, education, welfare and empowerment programs that uplift lives and strengthen social harmony.

Location: No. 6, Road 2, Ogbatomi, Omi Adio, Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria

Aims and Objectives

  • Establish, support and maintain a mosque for Islamic worship, teaching and community development.
  • Provide humanitarian and welfare services such as food support, medical outreach, shelter assistance and other relief programs for the public benefit.
  • Organise seminars, workshops, religious programmes and community development activities that promote peace, moral values and social harmony.
  • Empower Muslims through skill acquisition, vocational training, business support and other livelihood initiatives that reduce poverty and promote self‑reliance.
